# req(ems): an effort with a specific start time sorts before an effort with no time

## Job Story

When I look at a list of efforts for a day, I want the ones I scheduled at a
specific clock time to appear before the unscheduled (all-day) ones, so my next
timed commitment is at the top.

## Statement (Gherkin)

```gherkin
Given two efforts, one with a specific start time (e.g. 09:00:00)
  and one at midnight 00:00:00 (no specific time)
When the efforts are sorted by start time
Then the effort with the specific time orders before the no-time effort
And the no-time effort orders after it
```
